How it plays (Pass Line)

Bet the Pass Line. The first roll is the come-out roll.

Come-out roll

  • 7 or 11 → Pass Line WIN (pays 1.25× total).
  • 2, 3, or 12 → CRAPS (you lose, round ends).
  • 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 → That number becomes the Point. Your stake stays up.

Point phase

  • Roll until either the Point is made (you WIN 1.00×) or a 7 appears (SEVEN OUT, you lose).
  • Any other total is a neutral roll — no payout; stake remains.

Payouts

  • Come-out WIN (7/11) — 1.25× total
  • Point WIN — 1.00× total
  • JACKPOT — 2.00× total (random upgrade on any WIN)
  • CRAPS / SEVEN OUT — lose
